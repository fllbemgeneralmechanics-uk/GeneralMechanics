What we do best

FLLBE General Mechanics is the modern metal workshop for doors, fences, windows, frames, and tailored engineering parts. We modify metals and glass with expert welding, machining, bending, and finishing so your idea becomes a durable, high-impact result.

Lathe machining

Precision turning for shafts, bushings, and bespoke components with clean finishes and tight tolerances. Ideal for retrofit parts and custom mechanical assemblies.

  • High accuracy metal turning
  • Custom production runs
  • Rapid prototype adaption

Milling and fabrication

Complex metal shaping, plate cutting, and profile milling that delivers strong custom structures for gates, doors, fences, and architectural metalwork.

  • Plate work and brackets
  • Precision profile milling
  • Weld-ready fabrication

Metal, glass, and engineered systems

Metal modification

We reshape steel, aluminium and other metals with cutting, bending, welding, and finishing to meet architectural and mechanical requirements.

Glass support systems

Precision framing and fitting for glass panels, windows, and storefront structures where strength and style must work together.

Engineering services

From design-ready consults to final assembly, we deliver engineered metal solutions with an emphasis on performance, durability, and modern execution.
